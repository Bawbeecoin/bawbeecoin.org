Introducing Bawbeecoin (BAW)

Bawbeecoin is an electronic currency based on Bitcoin, free from political wranglings and central bank control. Bawebeecoin, like Bitcoin is a digital, decentralised currency that can be created, spent, accepted and transferred anywhere, without fees or commission, anytime, globally, in real-time by anyone whenever and where ever they choose.

Bawbeecoin circumvents political, centralised and corporate control using a peer to peer network of people - your neighbour, a friend of a friend, someone you know, everyday people like you and me. Once established the network can't be turned off, shut down, manipulated or intervened by politicians or financial authorities that "know what's best for us".

Launching 1st April 2014

Bawebeecoin will be released 1st April 2014 and include Bawbeecoin-qt (aka BAW Bag), a user friendly wallet app and bawebeecoind, server based software for those that want to mine and build services around the coin. Please join our mailing list to be notified on launch schedule and follow our Twitter feed for up to date progress reports on development, testing and launch. Downloads will be available for Windows, Linux, OS X and source code available on Github.
